About loneliness in seniors


What would you do if you were in their situation?
Loneliness in seniors is frequently difficult to spot. Seniors themselves are often unaware they are lonely or may be reluctant to disclose that they are. Loneliness and social isolation are rarely considered or addressed in health and mental health care settings. Yet they are among the most modifiable risk factors for mortality and morbidity. Our goal is to equip you with the knowledge to make a change around you, by helping the isolated seniors you know, or come across.
