HOW CAN I HELP?

You can be a helping hand, not just through actively participating in your community, but through watching out for the elderlies that are in need of help. Just being self-aware of the language you use while talking to elderlies will help our community a lot. here are some basic tips to guide you through your interactions.

Tip #1: Be patient

Do not rush a conversation by dominating the conversation or by speaking quickly.

Speak at an appropriate pace. Listen patiently and give them the chance to talk.

Tip #2: Speak Clearly

Avoid mumbling and talking too quickly. Do not shout at them.

Enunciate clearly and loudly. Use short, simple words and sentences.

Tip #3: Don't be condescending

Don’t speak to them as if they are a child by using infantile language.

Be considerate of them but be careful not to patronize them.
